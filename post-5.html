Action verbs & metrics: 50 bullet starters

Sep 23, 2025 • Resumes

Use these plug-and-play stems to turn tasks into impact. Replace brackets with your details and numbers.

Formula

Action verb + what you did + how + result (with a number if possible)

50 bullet starters

  • Increased [metric] by [X]% by [action].
  • Reduced [cost/time] by [X]% by [action].
  • Launched [feature/campaign] that led to [result].
  • Optimized [process/tool] to improve [metric] by [X]%
  • Automated [task] saving [X] hours per [week/month].
  • Streamlined [process] cutting cycle time by [X]%
  • Developed [asset/system] used by [team/department].
  • Designed [UI/flow] increasing conversion by [X]%
  • Refactored [code/module] reducing errors by [X]%
  • Implemented [tool/practice] improving reliability to [X]%.
  • Negotiated [deal/contract] saving [$X] annually.
  • Coordinated [initiative] across [X] teams to deliver [result].
  • Led [project] from concept to launch in [X] weeks.
  • Piloted [experiment] that validated [learning].
  • Improved NPS/CSAT from [A] to [B] through [action].
  • Built dashboards to monitor [metric], enabling [decision].
  • Analyzed [dataset] to uncover [insight] that drove [action].
  • Cut churn by [X]% by [retention tactic].
  • Increased retention by [X]% via [program].
  • Scaled [process/system] to support [X] users/orders/month.
  • Created onboarding that reduced ramp time by [X] days.
  • Standardized documentation lowering handoff issues by [X]%
  • Conducted [user/customer] interviews to inform [decision].
  • Tested [A/B variant] improving CTR by [X]%
  • Revamped messaging increasing reply rate by [X]%
  • Improved SEO ranking for [keyword] from [#] to [#].
  • Grew organic traffic by [X]% via [strategy].
  • Increased email open rates to [X]% by [tactic].
  • Boosted demo-to-close rate by [X]% with [change].
  • Introduced QA checks reducing defects by [X]%
  • Reduced support tickets by [X]% by [self-serve resource].
  • Shortened sales cycle by [X]% via [playbook/process].
  • Improved time-to-hire by [X] days through [process change].
  • Mentored [X] teammates who achieved [result].
  • Trained [team] on [tool], increasing adoption to [X]%
  • Drove adoption of [feature] to [X]% MAU by [tactic].
  • Resolved [incident/outage] in [X] minutes by [action].
  • Eliminated [bottleneck] by [change], raising throughput [X]%
  • Rebuilt [component/process] cutting costs by [$X].
  • Secured [grant/partnership] worth [$X] with [proposal].
  • Localised content for [market] increasing signups by [X]%
  • Expanded to [new market/channel] generating [X] users.
  • Constructed financial model projecting [KPI] with [accuracy]% accuracy.
  • Improved accessibility score from [A] to [B] via [fixes].
  • Hardened security reducing vulnerabilities by [X]%
  • Introduced analytics event schema to track [funnel step].
  • Reduced page load time from [A]s to [B]s improving SEO.
  • Implemented CI/CD cutting deploy time by [X]%
  • Set up incident runbooks reducing MTTR by [X]%
  • Owned roadmap for [area], delivering [X] releases/quarter.
  • Presented findings to [audience], enabling decision on [initiative].
